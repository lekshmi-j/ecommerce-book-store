The Kite Runner by Khaled Hosseini is a powerful story of friendship, betrayal, and redemption set against the backdrop of a changing Afghanistan. It follows Amir, a privileged boy from Kabul, and his loyal friend Hassan, the son of his father's servant. After a heartbreaking betrayal, Amir grapples with guilt and seeks redemption years later when he returns to Taliban-ruled Afghanistan. The novel explores themes of loyalty, honor, and the heavy cost of personal choices. Deeply emotional and vividly written, *The Kite Runner* offers a poignant portrayal of human flaws and the enduring hope for forgiveness and healing.
